COMPANY HR POLICY DOCUMENT
============================

LEAVE POLICY:
- All employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- New employees must complete 6 months probation before taking leave
- Sick leave is 10 days per year, separate from annual leave
- Maternity leave is 26 weeks as per government regulations
- Paternity leave is 15 days for new fathers
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days

WORK FROM HOME POLICY:
- Employees can work from home maximum 2 days per week
- Work from home requires prior approval from direct manager
- Core working hours are 10am to 4pm IST regardless of location
- Employees must be reachable on phone and email during core hours

REIMBURSEMENT POLICY:
- Travel reimbursements must be submitted within 30 days of travel
- Food allowance is Rs 500 per day during business travel
- Hotel stay limit is Rs 3000 per night for domestic travel
- Flight tickets must be economy class for domestic travel
- All reimbursements require original receipts

PERFORMANCE REVIEW:
- Performance reviews happen twice a year — June and December
- Employees are rated on a scale of 1 to 5
- Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for promotion
- Salary increments are given once a year in April
