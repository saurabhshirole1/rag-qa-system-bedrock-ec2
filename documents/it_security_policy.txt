IT SECURITY POLICY
==================

PASSWORD POLICY:
- All passwords must be minimum 12 characters long
- Passwords must contain uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- Never reuse the last 5 passwords
- Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all company accounts

DEVICE POLICY:
- All company laptops must have antivirus software installed
- Operating system must be kept updated at all times
- Company data must never be stored on personal devices
- Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT within 1 hour
- Laptops must be locked when left unattended

DATA POLICY:
- Confidential data must never be sent via personal email
- Company documents must be stored on approved cloud storage only
- Customer data must not be shared with third parties without consent
- All data transfers outside the company must be encrypted

INTERNET USAGE:
- Company internet must be used for business purposes only
- Streaming services and social media are restricted during work hours
- Downloading software from unknown sources is strictly prohibited
- VPN must be used when accessing company resources remotely
